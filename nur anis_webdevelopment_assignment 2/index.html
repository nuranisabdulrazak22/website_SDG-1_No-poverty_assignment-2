About Us

This website was created to raise awareness about poverty in Malaysia and to support donation efforts for those in need. We believe everyone deserves access to food, safety, and basic necessities. Through collective donations, we help bring hope and support to vulnerable communities

Our Work

Our work focuses on supporting people living in poverty across Malaysia. All donations are directed towards food aid, basic necessities, and community support. We also run campaigns for schools, homeless individuals, and vulnerable communities, to raise awareness and provide direct assistance.

Resources

This section provides information about poverty in Malaysia, available support services, and ways to get help or contribute. Our resources aim to educate students and communities while encouraging meaningful action.
